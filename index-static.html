FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the warranty on your services? +

We offer a comprehensive 25-year manufacturer warranty on materials and a 5-year workmanship guarantee on all our services. This protects your investment and gives you complete peace of mind.

How long does a rendering project typically take? +

Project timelines vary depending on the scope and size. A typical house might take 2-4 weeks. Weather conditions may affect scheduling. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your consultation.

Do you offer free consultations? +

Yes! We provide free site assessments and consultations. Our team will evaluate your property, discuss your needs, and provide a detailed quote with no obligation.

What areas do you serve? +

We serve Sheffield and surrounding areas in South Yorkshire. Our team operates throughout the region covering properties from Dore to Grenoside and everywhere in between.

Are your materials environmentally friendly? +

We use high-quality, durable materials that are designed to last decades, reducing the need for frequent replacement. We're committed to sustainable practices in all our work.

Can you work on period properties? +

Absolutely! We have extensive experience with period properties and understand the specific requirements for listed buildings and heritage properties. We use appropriate materials and techniques for each property type.
