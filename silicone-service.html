Why Choose Silicone Rendering?

The Gold Standard in Durability

Silicone rendering represents the pinnacle of exterior finishing technology. Unlike traditional sand and cement renders that brittle and crack over time, silicone systems are engineered to move with your building—a crucial advantage in the UK's variable climate.

The self-cleaning hydrophobic surface repels water and prevents algae growth, meaning your home maintains its pristine appearance year after year with minimal maintenance. It's not just a coating; it's a comprehensive protection system.

We specialize in applying BBA-approved systems with manufacturer guarantees of up to 25 years, ensuring your investment is protected long-term.

How It Works

Silicone System Breakdown

Professional silicone rendering is a multi-layer system engineered for strength, flexibility, and longevity.

1. Base Coat Foundation

A fibre-reinforced polymer-modified base coat is applied directly to the substrate. This layer provides strength and ensures proper adhesion.

2. Alkali-Resistant Mesh

High-grade reinforcement mesh is embedded into the base coat. This allows the system to flex with building movement, preventing hairline cracks.

3. Levelling Coat

A thin levelling coat creates a smooth, uniform surface, ensuring consistent texture and finish quality.

4. Primer

Professional primer ensures optimal adhesion and colour intensity for the final topcoat.

5. Silicone Topcoat

The premium silicone finish provides the hydrophobic, self-cleaning, through-colored surface that defines the system's performance.

Texture Options

Choose from fine, medium, or coarse textures (typically 1.5mm). Texture affects water drainage, light play, and aesthetic appeal.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Will silicone rendering stop my house from cracking? +

Silicone render is highly flexible and we embed a structural alkali-resistant mesh into the base coat. This allows the render to move slightly with your building as it expands and contracts with temperature changes. This virtually eliminates the hairline cracks you see in traditional plastering.

Is it really "self-cleaning"? +

Yes. Silicone render is hydrophobic, meaning rainwater beads up and rolls off the surface, taking most dirt and dust with it. It significantly resists green algae growth, which is a common problem with older renders in damp UK climates. You'll notice a dramatic difference in maintenance requirements compared to traditional renders.

How long does silicone render actually last? +

When applied correctly as a full system, silicone render lasts 25-30 years. Unlike traditional sand and cement, it doesn't brittle with age. The colour is "through-colored"—built into the material—so it won't peel or flake off. This is why manufacturers offer 25-year warranties on full silicone systems.
