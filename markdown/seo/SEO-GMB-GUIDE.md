## 📍 Google My Business (GMB) - CRITICAL FOR RANKINGS

### **Action Items to Complete:**

1. **Update Schema in Your HTML** (File: index.html)
   - Find: `"streetAddress": "[Your Street Address]"`
   - Replace with your actual address from GMB
   - Find: `"postalCode": "[Your Postcode]"`
   - Replace with your postcode (e.g., "S10 4")

2. **Update Business Hours Section**
   - Contact page shows: Mon-Fri 8am-6pm, Sat 9am-1pm
   - Verify these match your GMB hours exactly

3. **Get Your GMB Profile URL**
   - Go to Google My Business
   - Find your business profile URL
   - Add to schema `"sameAs"` field

// REPLACE WITH: "streetAddress": "123 Main Street",

// REPLACE WITH: "postalCode": "S10 1AB",

// OPTIONAL - Add your full address:
